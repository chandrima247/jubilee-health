Key exclusions under the policy include:

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before applying.

How long does the application take?+
The application itself takes only a few minutes when your details are ready, and completed applications are generally reviewed within 3 working days.
Who can be covered under J-Care?+
J-Care is available to Ugandan residents on an individual or family basis and can include the main member, spouse, children, and other eligible legal dependants.
Are pre-existing and chronic conditions covered?+
Yes, they can be covered after full disclosure and the applicable waiting period. This also applies to congenital conditions and related treatment within the plan limits.
When does coverage start?+
Coverage starts after approval and payment, and you then receive confirmation together with your membership card and policy documents.
Can I choose maternity, dental, or optical on their own?+
Maternity is optional, while dental and optical are optional benefits that require outpatient cover to be selected as well.
How are inpatient and emergency claims handled?+
Inpatient treatment needs pre-authorization at least 48 hours before admission where possible, and emergency admissions should be reported within 48 hours with the medical card presented at the provider.
